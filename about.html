About Me

Hoda Amel Abdalla is female photographer out of Los Angeles, Chicago, and New York City. She began her early career on the west-coast and has had the experience photographing for premiere photography studios, top modeling agencies, high-production corporations, and editorial freelance work. Specializing in commercial, editorial, lifestyle photography, Hoda Amel Abdalla has been published internationally in production, post-production, and CG "photo-realistic" imagery.
